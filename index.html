About

The Filmmaker

Griffin Meyer is a New York City-based video producer and cinematographer with over 10 years of experience turning complex spaces, brands, and moments into stories that move.

As Video Producer at SERHANT. — one of the most recognized real estate brands in the world — Griffin leads production from concept through delivery for luxury property listings, agent profiles, and brand campaigns. His work blends cinematic craftsmanship with the pace and precision that high-stakes commercial content demands.

Outside SERHANT., Griffin operates White Kid Black Camera Inc. (WKBC), his own production company, serving brands and artists across music, fashion, hospitality, and events. He's shot everything from packed concert halls to intimate runway shows, always with the same eye for light, motion, and moment.
